Same Simple Different Design

Initial research

Many of todays weather applications tend to have standard features that often confuse users. As such, the approach and goal of this app is looking at a simple yet effective approach at relaying the required weather information to its uers. With a unique twist that sets it appart differently from your standard everyday weather app.

App Layout Concept

The introduction of a suggestion feature, enables the app to communicate to the user and offer suggestions based on the current weather information given. It will vary also based on Personalized information the users implements.

  • Basic weather information
  • Personalized suggestions
  • Real time update feature
  • Aesthetic Mood feature

    • App Features

    Personalized weather

    information can be updated by changing the settings region and date area. I personally wanted to add more options for more adaptability, but i still need more information about that aspect. Further research into weather metrics and how this information could be relayed would be an interesting add on.

    Suggestion feature

    Gives the user subtle tips and general suggestions to different weather ocassions, and this tips can be sent via text if the user wants too, were internet connection is unavailable. This will require handling whether text charges will be applied per notification for every weather update the user receives offline.

    Mood Feature

    This feature is more of an aesthetic aspect of the design layout of what the app will look like, basically the apps current colour will automatically changed in theme based on the current weather ocassion/mood of the day. Aiming at the idea of an adaptive aesthetic based on the weathers information.

    Further Potential Development

    The main idea and goal of this app was looking into the elements of a simple and straightforward weather application. Approaching with a feature that introduces suggestions to the user as subtle tips. Looking at how this app can go beyond its concept into development. Is this human element that it provides, giving this app an interesting feature that i really want to develop. Since not many modern weather applications today, have this element. Thou mostly the app is still reliant with personalized weather information, it also relies with what information is given, in order to come up with suggestions based on the weather information.

    Maybe as an add on, the suggestion feature can also be further enchanced to be more than just a simple tips and tricks informant everytime the weather changes. As such maybe elements such as adding a built in roadmap guide log. Which shows the user possible sheltered routes with buildings they can walk underneath. Provided of course they want to walk and not take a bus or ride to their destination. Thou an obvious suggestion, with further development adaptability with information is key to adding more detailed suggestions. Mainly targeting the aspect for users, who arent aware of the sudden weather chancge.

    This element of suggestion in a weather app, is the main goal i wanted to present into this app, stepping it away from normal everyday weather information, and turning it into a unique brand of personalize, interactivity through tips and suggestions.
